About Me

My journey as a software engineer began back in 2019 while trying to hack the Bulgarian government with Python. Needless to say, I was deeply disappointed when I realized it wouldn't be sufficient to just type "Hello Universe" to gain access to the country and remove my parking fines. So I moved on and tried out something less demanding like a Tic-Tac-Toe. That is when I finally understood that I couldn't call myself an engineer if I can fix my grandma's TV or restart her Internet. Because of these facts, my adventure continues to this day...

Other than that I'm currently in university studying Finance and Economics at New Bulgarian University in Sofia. I was a Tech lead and Software Engineer at xtingles LTD. I am from Bulgaria and living with my amazing girlfriend in the capital Sofia. I have a love-hate relationship with ice cream and in my spare time, I like playing basketball, reading books, and spending time with friends and family but also alone.
